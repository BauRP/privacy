TRIVO CHAT: DATA PRIVACY PROTOCOL

Official Document for Google Play Store Compliance

I. INTRODUCTION & CORE VALUES

Trivo Chat is a decentralized, peer-to-peer (P2P) communication platform. Our core mission is total user sovereignty. We operate under a Zero-Knowledge policy, meaning we do not have technical access to your private conversations or metadata.

II. INFORMATION COLLECTION & USE

We believe that the best way to protect your data is to never collect it.

  • Personal Data: We do NOT require or store phone numbers, email addresses, or real names.
  • Contact Lists: We do NOT upload your contacts to any server.
  • Cookies: Our application does not use tracking cookies or advertising identifiers.

III. DATA STORAGE & ARCHITECTURE

Trivo Chat does not utilize central servers for message storage. All data is managed as follows:

  • Local Sovereignty: Your chat history, encryption keys, and media files are stored strictly on your physical device.
  • Decentralization: Messages are delivered directly from sender to receiver. No copies are kept on any cloud infrastructure.

IV. ENCRYPTION & SECURITY

Every communication channel in Trivo Chat is protected by end-to-end encryption using PQXDH (Post-Quantum) protocols. This ensures that only the intended participants can read the content, protecting it from any third-party interception.

V. DATA RETENTION & DELETION

You have 100% control over your data. Deleting a chat or the entire application results in the immediate and permanent destruction of all associated data from your device. We cannot recover deleted data because we never possessed it.

VI. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Trivo Chat does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Given our anonymous nature, we encourage parents to supervise their children’s digital activities.

VII. COMPLIANCE (GDPR & CCPA)

We respect international privacy standards. Under GDPR and CCPA, users have the right to access and delete their data. In Trivo Chat, these rights are exercised by the user directly through the application's local management tools.

VIII. CONTACT INFORMATION

For technical inquiries regarding this protocol, please contact the development team through our official GitHub repository or support channels.
