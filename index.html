TRIVO CHAT: DATA PRIVACY PROTOCOL

Official Document for Google Play Store Compliance

I. DECENTRALIZED MODERATION (UGC POLICY)

Trivo Chat empowers users to maintain a safe environment through a built-in Reporting System. Each user has the right to flag content that violates our Community Guidelines.

Standard Reporting Categories:

  • 1. Spam or Scams: Commercial advertising or deceptive financial practices.
  • 2. Harassment or Bullying: Targeted personal abuse or intimidation.
  • 3. Child Safety: Any content involving the harm or exploitation of minors.
  • 4. Illegal Activities & Extremism: Promotion of terrorism or criminal acts.
  • 5. Hate Speech: Attacks based on race, religion, or gender.
  • 6. Other: Miscellaneous policy violations.

CRITICAL ENFORCEMENT PROTOCOL:

We apply a Zero-Tolerance Policy for the following high-priority violations:

  • PRIORITY 3 CHILD SAFETY: Any Peer ID associated with the exploitation of minors will be PERMANENTLY BANNED from the relay network immediately.
  • PRIORITY 4 ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES & EXTREMISM: Promotion of terrorism, organized crime, or violent extremist ideologies results in an IRREVERSIBLE TERMINATION of network access.

*Enforcement Action: Banned users lose all P2P handshake capabilities. Access cannot be restored by the development team.

II. PRIVACY & DATA SOVEREIGNTY

Our P2P architecture ensures that we do NOT collect or store your private conversations. All data is managed locally on your physical device.

III. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES (ADMOB)

Trivo Chat integrates Google AdMob. AdMob may utilize advertising identifiers (AAID) and cookies to provide ad services. This is subject to Google’s standard Privacy Policy.
