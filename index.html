TRIVO CHAT: DATA PRIVACY PROTOCOL

Official Document for Google Play Store Compliance

I. INTRODUCTION & CORE VALUES

Trivo Chat is a decentralized, peer-to-peer (P2P) communication platform. We operate under a Zero-Knowledge policy: no technical access to private data or metadata.

II. INFORMATION COLLECTION & USE

We do NOT collect phone numbers, emails, or real names. Data storage is 100% local on your device.

IX. USER CONDUCT & CONTENT MODERATION (UGC POLICY)

To ensure a safe environment, Trivo Chat employs a Community-Based Reporting System. Users can report violations via the "Report" button in chat settings.

  • 1. Spam or Scams: Deceptive practices or automated advertising.
  • 2. Harassment: Targeted abuse or bullying.
  • PRIORITY 3 CHILD SAFETY: Any content involving the exploitation of minors.

    Status: Strictly Monitored. Penalty: IMMEDIATE & PERMANENT BAN.

  • PRIORITY 4 ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES & EXTREMISM: Promotion of terrorism or organized crime.

    Status: Strictly Monitored. Penalty: IMMEDIATE & PERMANENT BAN.

  • 5. Hate Speech: Discrimination based on race, religion, or gender.

ENFORCEMENT: Violations of Priority 3 and 4 are handled with Zero Tolerance. Upon verification of multiple reports, the associated Peer ID is permanently blacklisted from the network relay nodes without the possibility of recovery.

X. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & ADVERTISING

Trivo Chat uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (AAID) and cookies to serve ads. This process is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

XI. DATA DELETION

Deleting the application or a specific chat results in the permanent destruction of all local data. We cannot recover what we never stored.
