I. Core Data Protocol

Trivo Chat is built on the principle of Zero-Knowledge. We do not operate central servers, which means we cannot intercept, store, or sell your personal information. Every message is a direct hand-shake between peers.

II. Official Privacy Policy

1. DATA MINIMIZATION: We do not ask for, collect, or process: Phone numbers, Email addresses, Physical locations, or Contact lists. Identity is generated locally on your device.

2. PEER-TO-PEER (P2P): All data exchange occurs directly between user devices via WebRTC/DHT. No intermediary relays have access to unencrypted payloads.

3. STORAGE: Chat history and metadata exist ONLY within the application's local sandbox on your hardware. If you delete the app, the data is gone forever.

4. THIRD PARTIES: Since no data is collected, no data can be shared with advertisers, governments, or third-party entities.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY: Our protocol is designed for total anonymity and does not target or identify users by age.

6. GLOBAL COMPLIANCE: We adhere to the spirit of GDPR and CCPA by ensuring the user has 100% control over their digital footprint via local data management.
