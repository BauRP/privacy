TRIVO CHAT: PRIVACY ARCHITECTURE & SECURITY PROTOCOL

Global Standard for Decentralized Communication // Version 2.2.0 (2026)

I. CORE ARCHITECTURAL SECURITY

Trivo Chat is not a service; it is a Decentralized Protocol. Our security model is based on the principle of "Trustless Communication," where the system is designed to be secure even if the underlying network is compromised.

01. P2P Handshake Connections are established directly between devices. There is no central authority to intercept, monitor, or block traffic.
02. Post-Quantum Cryptography We employ PQXDH (Post-Quantum Extended Diffie-Hellman) for key exchange, ensuring security against future quantum computing attacks.
03. Zero-Knowledge Proofs The developers have technical 0% access to your identity, contact list, or data packets. Your identity is a cryptographic hash, not a database entry.
04. Local Sovereignty Encryption keys and chat history never leave your physical hardware. You are the sole custodian of your digital footprint.

II. USER-GENERATED CONTENT (UGC) & REPORTING

To comply with Google Play Store policies and international safety standards, Trivo Chat includes a Community-Based Moderation System. Users can flag peers for violating safety standards using the following categories:

  • 01. Spam & Scams
  • 02. Harassment & Bullying
  • 03. Child Safety
  • 04. Illegal Activities & Extremism
  • 05. Hate Speech
  • 06. Other
ZERO TOLERANCE: PRIORITY 3 & 4

Trivo Chat enforces an automated Permanent Blacklisting protocol for the most severe violations to ensure global network health:

  • Child Safety (Priority 3): Any verified attempt to exploit minors results in an immediate, network-wide ban of the Peer-ID.
  • Extremism (Priority 4): Promotion of terrorism or violent extremist ideologies leads to irreversible cryptographic termination of service access.

*Sanctions are executed at the relay node level, making re-entry technically impossible for the flagged device ID.

III. DATA DELETION & ADVANCED PROTECTION

Panic Trigger (Instant Wipe)

Trivo Chat includes a "Panic Mode." Users can trigger an Instant Data Wipe within the application, which permanently overwrites local encryption keys and chat databases, rendering all information unrecoverable even by forensic tools.

No Metadata Logging

Our relay nodes operate in "Blind Mode." They do not log IP addresses, timestamps, or routing patterns. We prioritize the anonymity of the connection as much as the content of the message.

IV. ADVERTISING & TRANSPARENCY (ADMOB)

Trivo Chat utilizes Google AdMob for monetization. AdMob may collect device-specific information (Android Advertising ID) and cookies to serve advertisements. This process is strictly separate from the encrypted chat environment and is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

V. LEGAL COMPLIANCE & JURISDICTION

As a decentralized P2P tool, Trivo Chat does not act as a data controller under GDPR or CCPA. We provide the infrastructure; users are responsible for the content they transmit. We fulfill legal obligations by providing tools for community moderation while maintaining the technical impossibility of decrypting user traffic.
