[ TRIVO PRIVACY PROTOCOL V1.0 ● ]

DATE STAMP: March 26, 2026

Trivo Chat operates on a pure Zero-Knowledge Architecture. No user data is stored on central servers. Communication is Peer-to-Peer (P2P).

1. DATA MINIMIZATION

We explicitly do NOT collect: Phone numbers, Email addresses, Names, GPS/Location, or Contact Lists. No central user directory exists.

2. P2P & LOCAL STORAGE

All decryption keys and chat histories are stored ONLY on your local device. Deleting the application or clearing cache permanently destroys all data.

3. ENCRYPTION PROTOCOL

Trivo Chat utilizes PQXDH (Post-Quantum) encryption for all channels, ensuring security against future computational threats.

4. USER RIGHTS (GDPR/CCPA)

Users exercise their 'Right to be Forgotten' by manually deleting local app data. Due to our serverless nature, we possess no data to share with third parties.
